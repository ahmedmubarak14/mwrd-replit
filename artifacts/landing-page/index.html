What is mwrd and who is it for?

mwrd is the first AI-powered B2B procurement marketplace in Saudi Arabia. It supports small businesses, mid-market teams, enterprises, and semi-government entities by connecting them with verified suppliers across more than eight categories. If your company buys or sells in Saudi Arabia, mwrd is built for you.

How does blind bidding work?

When you publish a purchase request, suppliers submit their bids without seeing each other. Buyers also do not see supplier identities during the bidding stage. That keeps competition clean and pricing honest. Identities are only revealed after the winning bid is selected.

How are suppliers verified?

The mwrd team verifies every supplier directly before activation. You only work with licensed and trusted Saudi companies, without having to run the process yourself.

Is mwrd aligned with Saudi regulatory requirements?

Yes. mwrd is designed from the ground up around Saudi regulatory expectations, which gives enterprises and semi-government entities far more confidence in adopting it.

Which categories does mwrd cover?

Today the platform supports more than eight categories including office supplies, IT and electronics, food and beverage, furniture, facilities management, raw materials, corporate gifting, and logistics services. More are added over time.
